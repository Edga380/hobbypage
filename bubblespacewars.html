Bubble Space Wars

Action | Casual | Arcade
Bubble Space Wars is a Free to play game where you battle other bubbles.
All enemy bubbles and bosses have unique skills so watch out!
As long as you level up your own skills you will have no problem defeating them.

The game is completely Free To Play and isn't Pay To Win and not going to be.

Key features:
- Unlimited levels.
- Google Play Leader board.
- Unique player skins.
- 10 unique enemy bubbles.
- 5 unique bosses.
- Daily quests.
- Daily login rewards.
